Sources and corrections

Last reviewed Jun 8, 2026 - Plan year 2026

Source-first rule: ACA subsidy pages should point readers toward official IRS, HHS, CMS, and HealthCare.gov materials whenever a rule, threshold, or final Marketplace action matters.

Primary source types

  • HealthCare.gov for official Marketplace enrollment and final plan confirmation.
  • HealthCare.gov CSR guidance for cost-sharing reduction basics.
  • HHS/ASPE poverty guidelines for federal poverty guideline references.
  • IRS materials for premium tax credit and reconciliation concepts.

Corrections policy

If an official rule, source page, estimate assumption, or internal guide path is inaccurate or stale, the affected page should be corrected and the reviewed date should be updated. Corrections should preserve reader safety by explaining limits rather than overstating certainty.

Estimate limitations

Calculator and article examples are planning tools. They do not replace HealthCare.gov, filed plan data, tax filing instructions, or professional advice for a specific household.

Correction request path

For correction request details, use the contact page. The public contact channel will be finalized when the production domain is assigned.
