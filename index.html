Methodology snapshot

What this estimate does and does not do

The calculator estimates the benchmark Silver plan premium, the federal poverty level percentage, and the premium tax credit under two rule sets. Rates are illustrative for implementation and must be replaced with filed 2026 CMS data before consumer launch.

This is not a quote, not enrollment, and not insurance, tax, or legal advice. Confirm actual coverage and pricing at HealthCare.gov. See the full Florida ACA subsidy methodology.
